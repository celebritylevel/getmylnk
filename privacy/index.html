Last updated: April 10, 2026

Welcome to GetMyLnk ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at getmylnk.com (the "Site").

We may collect the following types of information:

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Aggregated, anonymized analytics data may be shared with creators whose pages you visit.

Creator pages contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies separately.

5. Data Retention

Analytics data (page views, clicks, country) is retained in aggregate, anonymized form. We retain contact form submissions for up to 12 months.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at hi@getmylnk.com.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your information. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Site and creator pages are intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18.
